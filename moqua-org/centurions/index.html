About the Award

The OA Centurion Award

For nearly 100 years, the Order of the Arrow has purposefully recognized Scouts and Scouters who best exemplify the Scout Oath and Law in their daily lives. The Centurion Award aims to highlight "Hometown Heroes" — Arrowmen who have meaningfully contributed to the forming, maturing, and ongoing operational excellence of their local council's lodge, and who, in doing so, inspired others to follow in their footsteps.

The organization's centennial provided a unique opportunity to commemorate those among us who are exemplars of the "high ideals and purpose of the Order of the Arrow." Owasippe Lodge #7 is proud to recognize seven such members.
