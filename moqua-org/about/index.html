Mission & History

Leadership in Service

"The watchwords of the Vigil Honor — a call to even greater service to others."
Order of the Arrow · Vigil Honor

The Moqua Foundation honors the men of Owasippe Lodge #7 who received the Vigil Honor — Scouting's highest recognition within the Order of the Arrow, awarded to those who have rendered distinguished service to Scouting and demonstrated an exceptional commitment to cheerful service.

Owasippe Lodge #7 has operated in support of the Chicago Area Council, Boy Scouts of America since 1921 — making it one of the oldest continuously operating OA lodges in the country. The lodge is home to Owasippe Scout Reservation, a 4,000-acre property in Michigan that has shaped the lives of hundreds of thousands of Chicago-area Scouts.

The creators of this site hope it will serve as a rallying point for Vigil Honor members past and present to use their collective talents to promote a love of the outdoors and a sense of obligation to serve society as a whole.
